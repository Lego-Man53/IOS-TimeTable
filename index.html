Install on your Home Screen

  1. Open this app link in Safari on your iPhone.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open My Jamea Timetable from your Home Screen.

After the first load, the app files are cached so you do not need to keep downloading it again.
